Reports

Generate commission reports for the current pay period. Reports open in a new tab for printing and are saved to Google Drive.

How reports work

Each report shows all sales currently on the books that have not been fully paid. Items marked X in Comm Paid or Ovg Paid are included in the period totals. After generating, the report opens in a new tab where you can print or save as PDF. A copy is also saved to your Google Drive folder named Commission Reports with the filename format: Salesperson Name — Period, Year.
